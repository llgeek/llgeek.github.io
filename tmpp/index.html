Biography

Greetings! My name is Linlin (Liam) Chen. I am now a third year Ph.D student at Department of Computer Science, Illinois Institute of Technology(IIT). I am supervised by Prof. Xiang-Yang Li and Prof. Peng-Jun Wan. My research interests span in data privacy & security, deep learning, machine learning, mobile computing. I like taking challenges and solving practical problems.

I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree from the School of Computer Science, University of Science and Technology of China(USTC).

Please find my C.V. here.

I am now looking for both research and SDE interns related to data engineering, deep learning, mobile computing.
