Biography

Greetings! My name is Linlin Chen. I am now a fourth year Ph.D candidate at Department of Computer Science, Illinois Institute of Technology(IIT). I am supervised by Prof. Xiang-Yang Li and Prof. Peng-Jun Wan. My research interests span in data privacy & security in deep learning and machine learning. I like taking challenges and solving practical problems.

I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree from the School of Computer Science, University of Science and Technology of China(USTC), 2015.

Please find my C.V. here.

I am now actively looking for both research and SDE interns related to applied data science, deep learning, machine learning and natural language processing.
